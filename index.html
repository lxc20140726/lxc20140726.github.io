Hi, l'm Fred Butcher
From SNNU major in cs

A rookie who just founded this site

My name is Xing chen, meaning stardust in the cosmos, which happens to be one of the things l adored most. It amazed me every time that we are composed by the same particles, which have travelled in this universe through billions of years, forming other spectacular wonders, maybe life. My English name is, on the other hand, Fred Underwood, or Butcher. You can just call me Simple, who is one of my favorite CSGO player with much talents. For the record, l love playing video games such as DOTA2, CSGO, the list goes on and on, however, l won’t spend too much time on it. As you can see, my English name comes usually from TV series and films’ characters. ‘Underwood’ came from House of cards. ‘Butcher’ came from THE BOYS, not to mention the greatest cartoon in my heart Rick and Morty. On the one hand, l love games and watching stuff. On the other hand, sports, especially soccer, plays a fundamental part in my life. It’s so important that without which, l couldn’t be writing this. Getting a goal in football matches feels good, of course. but what causes it, team work, is much more critical, and it does for any kinds of sports. I am a person, which means l can be rational or emotional in different occasions, maybe a little bit lean to rational. I am also a positive and outgoing boy, giving me lots of chance to communicate with others in either English or Chinese, thus enabling me to have a better grasp of English talking. Despite the fact that I’ve already gained several prizes involving English, such as Shanghai Science English Contest, it’s always important to maintain a humble spirit and keep learning. So, the only thing l’m sure of myself is that I don’t know everything.
